Solar Basic - Essentail Hardware & Balance of System

The essential hardware for a solar PV system are the solar panels and an inverters. The solar panels are required to capture the solar energy and the inverter is to convert the DC energy to AC energy. The balance of system (BOS) of a solar power system refers to all the components that aren't the solar panels themselves, but are still essential for the system's functionality and longevity. BOS components include: wiring, switches, mounting system, solar inverters, battery bank, enclosures, fuses, and ground fault detectors.
