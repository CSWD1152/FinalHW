Planning for Residential Solar

There are a numbers of steps to follow when planning to power your home with solar energy. After choicing which option is best for your solar system, folloing the steps that applies to you. In general there are 6 basic steps that need to be followed through.

Site Survey

Assessing the solar potential of your site and to determine the best location for placement of solar panels in order to capture more solar insolation

System Design

Performing an energy audit on how much energy is needed and then provide a detail PV system design.

Pemitting

For a grid tie solar syetem, permit from the AHJ is required.

System installation

Once the system design has been approved, solar installer can start putting the system together.

Inspection

Inspector from AHJ to inspect the installation is done per design.

Utilitity Connection

Grid connection is required prior to Issurance of Permit to Operate by the utility company.
