Residential Solar (Photovoltaic) Electric System

Solar energy is the most abundant energy source on Earth. It is sustainable, renewable, and plentiful. It plays an important role in reducing greenhouse gas emissions and mitigating climate change, which is critical to protecting humans, wildlife, and the ecosystems. As the cost of electricity generated from non-ecofriendly fossil fuel or coal are rising while the cost of solar panels and the balance of system equipment are falling, it is about time to implement this technology on every residential home. Other benefits of going solar are:

  1. * Lower Utility Bill
  2. * Utility power independency
  3. * Power Outage Protection Option
  4. * Increased Home Value
